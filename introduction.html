How to play

When the game starts, each player will control a snake at a fixed starting position.

The head of the snake continually moves forward, unable to stop, growing ever longer when you eat the appple.

It has to turn left, right, up or down to avoid hitting the wall.

If you hit the wall you lose

The more apples you eat, the higher your score will be, try to get the highest score.

Good luck!
